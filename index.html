Installation

1

Download

Click the download button above to get CoA_Addons.zip

2

Extract

Unzip the file and copy the 3 addon folders into your WoW directory:

World of Warcraft / Interface / AddOns /
3

Enable

Launch WoW → click AddOns on the character select screen → tick all three CoA addons → log in.

4

Configure

Type /coat in game to open the settings panel and pick your class/spec.
